Rules of the Game

Instructions:

1. The deck contains 48 cards of 4 diferent types such as:
Sword , Gold , Cup , Club(Stick depending on the actual translation to english)

2. Before begining the game a random card is chosen from the deck to be the one that trumps all other types that game.

3. Each player begins with 3 cards on their hand.

4. After having their hand each player use one card each turn until no Card remains on the deck.

5. At the end each player counts the cards that have value and who ever has the highest point wins. The values for each card is:
One values: 11, Three values: 10, Ten values: 2, Eleventh values: 3, Twelthe values: 4, Others value: 0.
